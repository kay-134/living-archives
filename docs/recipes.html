Cornmeal Porridge Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water separated
  • 3 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup fine yellow cornmeal
  • 1 ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ¼ teaspoon ground all-spice
  • ½ cup sweetened condensed milk or to taste

Instructions

  1. Add 2 cups water, 3 1/2 cup milk to a 4 QT heavy bottomed pot. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat with vanilla extract, cinnamon, bay leaf, and all-spice.

  2. Meanwhile, add 1 cup cornmeal and 1 cup water to a large mixing cup and whisk until smooth.

  3. Once the pot begins to boil, whisk in cornmeal mixture and continue to whisk for about 1 minute, ensuring there are no lumps. Reduce heat to low and cover with a tight fitting lid.

  4. Once the pot begins to boil, whisk in cornmeal mixture and continue to whisk for about 1 minute, ensuring there are no lumps. Reduce heat to low and cover with a tight fitting lid.

  5. Cook over low heat for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. When there is about 5 minutes left,. Remove from heat and sweeten with condensed milk. Serve and enjoy.
